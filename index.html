About Mama Tooba Hair Products

Mama Tooba hair products are made from a combination of six herbs, uniquely blended into shampoo, conditioner, 3 in 1 hair food, hair oil and setting lotion. The products support quick, strong, long and healthy-looking hair and are used for common hair and scalp concerns such as receding edges, breakages, dandruff, itchy scalp, hair loss, thinning, baldness and unpleasant scalp odour. Suitable for natural, permed and dreadlocks.
